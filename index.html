How we met

They met at on a spring break camping trip at the Ozarks when Timo was a freshman, Shay a sophomore. It was March, and somehow camping didn't seem like a bad idea at the time...but it was the coldest they have ever been. Shay only remembers Timo as the guy who got kicked by his brother in the middle of the night- filling the entire forest with his scream. Timo doesn't remember that.

How we started dating

Shay wanted to get a meal with all her friends before graduation, and Timo was on the list. Coincidentally, Timo ran out of meal points so they had to cook. Timo also had a sprained ankle, but insisted that he can jog to Schnucks to get groceries. Shay didn't think that was very wise, so she accompanied him in her roommate's car. They cooked Shay's 5 months old steak, carbonara, and a side of broccoli. Her roommates came and went... wondering why they're still eating lunch at 5pm. Weeks later (with many texts and snapchats in between), Timo asked Shay out to dinner at Blueberry Hill on the Loop where it all became official.

Ugh... dress codes?

Mehndi 1PM - 5PM

Ethnic indian wear
Ladies can wear something bright & vivid and gentlemen can be on their vibrant kurtas with pants or pyjamas along with an optional shawl.

Cocktail Night 7PM - 12AM

Western attire
A semi-formal look that is contemporary & fashionable which is not too prim & proper is the best to pull off this night. You can see some pins for ladies & gents.

Wedding 6PM-12AM

Indo-western
Ladies can flaunt their unconventional sarees, lehengas, anarkalis, dresses and the list goes on whereas gentlemen can wear any kind of suit or just blazer. If you don't like to wear a suit/blazer, you can do a high-neck (band gala) waistcoat & shirt combo.
